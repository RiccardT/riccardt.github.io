What I do

Full Stack Web Development

I have been tinkering with web development since junior year of college and it continues to grab my attention to this day. I have developed web applications using HTML/CSS/JS and Node.js with Express and SQL for server and database management, respectively. Currently going through Angela Yu's excellent Udemy course on Web Development to round out my knowledge. At work I dabble in the Spring Java Framework for the back end and Angular for the front end.

Machine Learning

I have been studying and working with Machine Learning model pipelines with Python since senior year of college, and it is an area of software engineering that fascinates me greatly. I have some slight work experience from a team I was briefly on that worked with a natural language processing pipelines. Currently working through the Udemy course "Complete Machine Learning and Data Science: Zero to Mastery" by Andrei Neagoie and Daniel Burke to supplement my knowledge.

iOS Full Stack

My first foray into mobile development was with the open source cross platform mobile framework being developed by Google, "Flutter". Since then, I've realized that the proper path to mobile mastery was excellence in the native space first. I chose iOS because of the Swift language and the excellent mobile ecosystem that Apple has developed. I'm currently working through Angela Yu's excellent Udemy course on iOS engineering.

My work

Who I am

Designer & Developer based out of Columbus, Ohio

I am a Mexican born immigrant from the gorgeous city of Guadalajara, Mexico. I was raised in South Texas, and attended college there at the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley. I caught the software bug late into my mechanical engineering undergraduate career through the blog of my unofficial mentor at the time, Haseeb Qureshi. He painted a career in my mind of invention and impact that was so engaging, I had to give it a shot despite graduation looming in the distance. I started with some tutorials on the Ruby programming language on my spare time and from then on was pretty rapidly hooked, I'm happy to say!

Fast forward 2 years, and I'm now a professional software engineer at JP Morgan Chase & Co. The gratitude I feel at this point can probably only be matched by how much I still don't know about this incredible field. Currently, my fascinations include Robert C. Martin's "Clean Code", and how I'm constantly following short of that blessed ideal, iOS engineering, full stack web development, and how I can actually put machine learning to work at my job/projects.
